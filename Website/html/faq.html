Application

We recommend students start looking at universities and begin the application process about 1 year prior to the start of the desired school year/semester. In general, Canadian universities are split into 2 semesters: the fall semester which runs from the begining of September to the mid December and the winter semester which runs from the begining of January to mid April. The deadlines to apply for the fall semester are often the previous March, so its important to leave time to prepare a good application and think about your options before then.
Unfortunately once an application deadline has passed there isn't anything we can do to bypass the admissions office at your desired university. We can however help you prepare an application for the next semester. To ensure everything goes smoothly we recommend contacting one of our agents.

Agents

As you may have already realized, applying as an international student is hard. Our agents have spent a number of years helping international students get to their Canadian school of dreams. They will arrange your visa, application and present you your options. As an international student you aren't always able to make the best decisions from abroad- our agents are well educated and know all the ins and outs of Canadian universities, cities and what your life would look like in them!
